About us

For more than 30 years we have been delivering world-class construction and we've built many lasting relationships along the way.

Thebox is a real estate development, construction and acquisition company based in New York City, driven by an unmatched commitment to quality and innovation. With more than nine million square feet of real estate in various stages of development and a portfolio that includes the record-breaking Walker Tower and Stella Tower, 626 First Avenue and 111 West 57th Street, Thebox is an industry leader that is reinventing the very process by which major architectural projects are designed and built .

Thebox is rare among leading real estate developers as it carries out all construction work on your own projects. With no competing interests, Thebox can collaborate freely with visionary architects in a collaboration that always challenges the firm with the desire to raise the bar for luxury development, no matter what.

Thebox team consists of professionals who specialize in residential, hotel and mixed-use projects and have impressive experience in architecture, construction, design and engineering, as well as extensive experience in large projects in New York and Miami. Every member of the Thebox team prides itself on exceptional design standards and ingenuity that is evident in each of their projects, as well as a quality that cannot be matched time that permeates every detail.
